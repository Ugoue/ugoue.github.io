Research

Research interests

Most of my research is in Graph theory, but I am also very interested in problems lying at the intersection of graph theory and other areas. In particular, I like concepts related to geometric group theory, coarse geometry or topology.

A few topics I (am trying to) work on: Cayley graphs, geometric group theory, canonical tree-decompositions, twin-width, universal covering, evasiveness of graph properties, fractional colouring of graphs, diameter-degree problem, group tilings.
