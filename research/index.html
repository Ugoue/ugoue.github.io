Research

Research interests

Most of my research is in Graph Theory, but I am also very interested in problems lying at the intersection of Graph Theory and other areas. In particular, I like concepts related to Geometric Group Theory, Coarse Geometry or Topology.

A few topics I (am trying to) work on: Cayley graphs, coarse graph theory, metric graph theory, cycle bases, geometric group theory, canonical tree-decompositions, twin-width, universal covering, evasiveness of graph properties, fractional colouring of graphs, diameter-degree problem, group tilings.
